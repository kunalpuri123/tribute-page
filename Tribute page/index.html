Biography

Chuck started his IT career with training from CBT Nuggets and earned his Cisco and CompTIA certifications through guidance from trainers like Cisco expert Jeremy Cioara and networking expert Keith Barker. In 2014, Chuck started a popular YouTube channel, NetworkChuck, that rapidly grew to more than 16,000 followers. Initially making the YouTube channel a technical resource for Cisco collaboration, Chuck shifted the channel’s focus toward IT career and certification preparation advice.
